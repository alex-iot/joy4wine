Placeholder note. A dry rosé from one of Málaga's most interesting small producers — Friedrich Schatz, a German-born winemaker who has been working the Serranía de Ronda for decades. This Syrah rosado is pale salmon in the glass, with a nose that leans strawberry, dried herbs, and a faint smokiness you don't expect at this price.

On the palate it's dry, medium-bodied, and lifted with good acidity. Clean finish. Excellent with grilled fish or just as a terrace bottle on a warm evening — which in Málaga means most of the year.

Would buy again.
