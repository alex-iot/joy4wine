Placeholder entry. La Vinoteca de Málaga sits just off the main pedestrian strip in the old town, tucked into a narrow street that rewards exploration. The shop stocks a well-curated mix of bottles from across Spain, with a notably strong section dedicated to Sierras de Málaga DO — rare for a shop of this size.

The owner is knowledgeable and approachable, happy to explain the difference between the various local DOs without making you feel like you've stumbled into a lecture. Prices are fair, and there's usually a handful of bottles open for tasting on a given afternoon.

Worth a visit if you're looking for something more than the usual supermarket shelf.
